About

Ever since my childhood, I've been an avid reader. At every chance, you'd find me engrossed in a book, sometimes venturing into books not typically meant for a child's eyes.

It was only recently that romance novels captivated me, with their tantalizing narratives pulling me in. The book that rekindled my passion for reading and introduced me to this genre was "Fierce King" by Sadie Kincaid. Not only did it ignite my love for romance tales, but it also inspired me to pen my own spicy mafia story. I invite you to delve into my world, and perhaps, find a spark of inspiration for yourself.

Books

  • Ties of Devotion

    In Ties of Devotion, step into the world of Emilia Flynn and Giovanni Rossi. A chance childhood encounter leads Emilia to the grand doors of the Rossi mansion, where she's taken under the wing of the family patriarch, Antonio Rossi

    As she grows alongside the Rossi heirs, Giovanni, Cristiano and Salvatore, her heart becomes entwined with the eldest, Giovanni. But can love truly flourish in the shadows of the mafia?

    Embark on a tale of passion, danger, and unwavering devotion as Emilia and Giovanni grapple with their feelings amidst a world of intrigue.
